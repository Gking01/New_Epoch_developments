About Us

At New Epoch Development (Pty) Ltd. a BBBEEE owned private company (Reg.No 2014/034003/07), we are passionate about construction and dedicated to delivering excellence in every project we undertake. With over 20 years of industry experience and a skilled team of professionals, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the construction industry. Our commitment to quality, attention to detail, and client satisfaction sets us apart. Whether it's a residential, commercial, or industrial project, we approach each endeavor with a focus on innovation, efficiency, and superior craftsmanship. Our goal is to bring our clients' visions to life while exceeding their expectations. . The entity was incorporated on the 25th february 2014. New Epoch Developments is registered with NHBRC, CIDB (2GB) and CSD.

What sets us apart is our client-centric approach. We firmly believe that successful projects begin with open communication, collaboration, and a deep understanding of our clients' needs. From the initial consultation to the final walkthrough, we work closely with our clients, ensuring their goals and objectives are at the forefront of every decision we make. We value transparency and integrity, providing regular updates, accurate timelines, and cost-effective solutions. Our team is committed to delivering projects on time and within budget, without compromising on quality. When you choose New Epoch Development (Pty) Ltd., you can trust us to handle every aspect of your project with professionalism, expertise, and a genuine dedication to your satisfaction.
